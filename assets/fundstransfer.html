Internal Funds Transfer

  • Select an account you had saved or specify an account number.
  • Enter the amount to transfer.
  • If you like save the account number with a name that you can easily select in future transactions.
  • Press submit to transfer funds.

External Funds Transfer

  • Enter recipient's first name.
  • Enter recipient's last name.
  • Select recipient's bank.
  • Select bank branch to transfer to.
  • Enter recipient's account number.
  • Enter amount to transfer.
  • Press submit to transfer funds.
