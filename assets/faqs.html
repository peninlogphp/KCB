Is my western union gold!card free?

Yes, your western union gold!card is free of charge.

How will I receive my gold!card?

If you apply for gold!card online you will immediately get a confirmation E-mail with your personal gold!card number which you will use for any future transactions. If you apply at point of sale, you will get your gold!card at the counter.

Can I still earn points if I didn’t bring my gold!card number while doing transaction?

Yes, your gold!points can be added to your gold!card after the transaction by calling the gold!card customer service and providing the MTCN, card number and the fee paid.
