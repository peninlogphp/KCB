Main Screen

Select a service by clicking on it or scrolling up or down then pressing select key when the one you would like to consume is highlighted.

For informational services like 'Balance' and 'Mini Statement', you are not required to enter the PIN every time as long as the application is not idle for more than 5 minutes. In case you entered the wrong PIN in your previous request, press your phone's menu key and select the "Reset PIN" option for you to be prompted to enter the correct one.
